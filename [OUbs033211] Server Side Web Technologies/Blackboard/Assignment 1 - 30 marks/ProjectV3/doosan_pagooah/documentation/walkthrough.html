Website Walkthrough

Welcome to the CloudNext website walkthrough! This video will take you through the key features and functionalities of the platform, helping you get familiar with everything from browsing products to managing your cart and placing orders. Let’s dive in!

1. Homepage Overview

  • The homepage is the central hub where you can explore the latest cloud engineering eBooks.

  • At the top, you'll find the navigation bar with options like:

    • Home: Returns you to the homepage.
    • Ebooks Catalogue: Explore a wide selection of eBooks.
    • Cart: View and manage the items you’ve added to your cart.
    • Sign In/Sign Out: Log in to your account or create a new one if you’re not signed in.

  • Below the navigation, the homepage highlights:

    • A featured slider with selected eBooks.
    • Categories like Best Sellers and New Releases, allowing you to quickly browse popular and newly added books.

2. Search and Filters

  • On the homepage or the Search Products page, you can use the search bar to look for specific eBooks.
  • Apply filters by:
    • Product Name: Type the name of the book you are searching for.
    • Category: Select a category from the dropdown.
    • Price Range: Enter a minimum and maximum price to filter books within a budget.

Once you hit Search, the results will display matching books with their descriptions, prices, and availability. You can also paginate through the results at the bottom of the page.

3. Product Pages

  • Each product card displays essential details, such as:
    • Title
    • Price
    • Description
    • Quantity available
    • Author
    • Publication Date

Clicking on a product lets you view additional details and add it to your cart.

4. Managing Your Cart

  • Once you've found a product, click Add to Cart to save it for checkout.
  • Head to the My Cart page by clicking on the cart icon in the navigation bar. Here you can:
    • View items in your cart with their total price.
    • Update quantities: Increase or decrease the number of items for each product.
    • Remove items if you change your mind.
    • Save items for later to purchase them at another time.

The Price Details panel updates in real-time, showing you the total cost and delivery charges (if applicable).

5. Admin Features (Product Management)

  • For admin users, there is a Product Management page where they can:
    • Create new products: Add new eBooks by entering details such as name, price, description, and upload images.
    • Edit existing products: Update any product's details, including stock quantities.
    • Delete products: Remove products from the catalogue when no longer available.
    • Use the CRUD operations (Create, Read, Update, Delete) to manage the product list efficiently.

Conclusion

That’s a quick tour of the CloudNext website! We hope this walkthrough gives you a good understanding of how to explore, manage your cart, and complete purchases on the platform. Be sure to check out the embedded YouTube video for a more visual guide through these features.
