About Me

Hello, I'm Doosan Pagooah! I'm a seasoned DevOps Engineer with over 10 years of experience under my belt. My passion for technology is a constant fire, fueling my drive to innovate and push boundaries. As a DevOps Engineer, I specialize in streamlining and automating operations, making sure systems are built to scale and run efficiently. I'm a strong believer in bridging the gap between operations and development teams. By fostering collaboration, I help create seamless production workflows that empower both sides.

In my experience, I've likely encountered and overcome various challenges related to automation, optimization, and integration. I'm confident in my ability to translate business needs into technical solutions that deliver real value.

Beyond the technical aspects, I bring:

  • A collaborative spirit: I thrive in team environments and believe that communication is key to success.
  • A problem-solving mindset: I approach challenges with a proactive and analytical approach.
  • A dedication to continuous learning: The tech landscape is ever-evolving, and I'm committed to staying ahead of the curve.

I'm confident that my skills and experience would be a valuable asset to any team.

La Sentinelle Ltd.

At La Sentinelle Ltd., I contribute as a Systems Engineer, ensuring the smooth operation of our critical IT infrastructure. 12-05-2012
Alteo Energy Ltd.

As a SCADA Engineer at Alteo Energy Ltd., I play a vital role in optimizing our power generation and ensuring grid stability. I'm responsible for programming, maintaining, and troubleshooting the Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) system. My expertise in data acquisition and control systems allows me to optimize plant performance, identify potential issues early on, and ensure the smooth, reliable flow of clean energy from Alteo to the national grid.
